Mission Statement 
Our mission is to tackle unemployment by supporting people who became at a disadvantage due to the pandemic to connect with small businesses.

What is RapidHire?

RapidHire is an online platform that strives to connect small businesses with the people who have been out of work due to COVID-19. RapidHire is here to help students/ interns or potential job seekers to get jobs and help small businesses to grow their business. It helps small business recruiters looking for skilled and talented job employees like a marketing specialist, social media manager, and many other different positions posted by the small business recruiters to help them grow their business online presence. Recruiters get matched with the best candidate for the job. Besides that, RapidHire also helps job seekers to get hired quickly to enhance their work experience and skill set by working at small business companies.
